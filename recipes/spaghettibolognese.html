Spaghetti Bolognese

Ingedients to Spaghetti Bolognese

  • Spaghetti 200g
  • Minced Meat 500g
  • Tomatoes
  • Basil
  • Parmasan
  • Onion 1

How to prepare the dish

  1. Chop onion fine and cook it for 2 minutes
  2. When cooked, add the minced meat and cook till done
  3. Add Tomatoes and let simmer for 15 minutes
  4. Meanwhile simmering, boil spaghetti according to package instructions
  5. When done, Bon appetit!
